Bio
A Residual Affinity is a Post Hardcore Punk outfit out of Boise, Idaho. Described as “Pretty Good” by Oshie Bichar of Beartooth and “Better than Average” by The Punk Rock MBA, This five piece has released two albums and performed alongside Hawthorne Heights, SeeYouSpaceCowboy, and Authority Zero. With heavy breakdowns, sappy emo lyrics and catchy choruses, A Residual Affinity continues to harass the American West with music made for your MySpace homepage.
